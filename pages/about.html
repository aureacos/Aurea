The Founders

Born from an unlikely friendship

We wanted beauty that was honest — that told you exactly what was in it, where it came from, and why it works. That's not radical. That's just respect.
Isabelle Moreau & Dr. Kenji Tanaka Co-Founders, Maison Auréa

Isabelle, a poet with a philosophy degree from the Sorbonne, and Dr. Kenji Tanaka, a cosmetic biochemist from Kyoto who had spent twelve years in pharmaceutical R&D — they met at a symposium on sustainable ingredient sourcing in Lyon and realised they were both searching for the same thing: beauty without apology.

They launched Maison Auréa in 2017 from a 40-square-metre atelier in the 6th arrondissement of Paris, with a single serum, a manifesto, and a refusal to compromise.

Our Journey

Eight years of devotion

2017 The Beginning

Maison Auréa is founded in a 40m² atelier in Paris's 6th arrondissement. Our first product — the Sérum d'Or — is formulated over 14 months.

2018 First Recognition

Sérum d'Or wins Allure's Best of Beauty. We are stocked in six Parisian boutiques and begin shipping internationally to 12 countries.

2019 Makeup Launch

Palette Royale and Velvet Foundation launch to critical acclaim. Vogue Paris calls us "the most exciting new beauty house in a decade."

2020 Sustainability Pledge

We transition to 100% recycled glass packaging and launch our refill programme. We plant one tree for every order placed — 85,000 trees so far.

2022 Global Expansion

Auréa opens flagship boutiques in London, New York, and Tokyo. We now ship to 40 countries. The collection grows to 68 formulas.

2025 The Soleil Collection

Our most ambitious collection yet — summer formulas with golden marigold, vitamin C, and Provençal lavender. The next chapter begins.

Our Commitments

What we stand for

🌿
Pure Ingredients

96% naturally-derived, ethically harvested from sustainable farms across 14 countries. No parabens, no sulfates, no compromise. We publish our full ingredient lists — always.

🐇
Cruelty Free Forever

Leaping Bunny certified since day one. Every product is tested only by consenting human volunteers. This is not a marketing position — it is a founding principle.

♻️
Circular Packaging

Our bottles use 80% recycled glass. Return any empty and we refill it at 40% off. We have prevented 12 tonnes of virgin glass from entering production since 2020.

✦
Radical Transparency

Every QR code links to sourcing stories, third-party lab results, and the human beings who harvested each ingredient. We hide nothing. We are proud of everything.
