Our Maison

Born from passion,
refined by science

Maison Auréa was founded in a small Parisian atelier, where a biochemist and a poet decided that luxury beauty should feel like both a scientific discovery and a homecoming.

Every product undergoes 18 months of meticulous formulation before it earns the right to bear our name. We collaborate with master botanists, Provençal harvesters, and ethicists to ensure every ingredient is as kind as it is transformative.

Our Commitments

What we stand for

🌿
Pure Ingredients

96% naturally-derived, ethically harvested from sustainable farms across 14 countries. No parabens, no sulfates, no compromise. Ever.

🐇
Cruelty Free Forever

Leaping Bunny certified since day one. Every product is tested only by consenting human volunteers. Always. No exceptions. Always.

♻️
Circular Packaging

80% recycled glass in every bottle. Return any empty and we refill it at 40% off. Beauty should leave this world more beautiful than it found it.

✦
Radical Transparency

Full ingredient disclosure, always. Every QR code links to sourcing stories, third-party lab results, and the human beings behind each formula.
